Background

Hi, I’m Luke Holroyd, the sole creator of Zephyrus. I’m 20 years young and an avid programmer with a passion for entrepreneurship, data analysis and of course, Starcraft II.

I’ve been playing Starcraft as a dirty Protoss (What can I say, I like lasers) for about 2.5 years and reached Masters league (Top 3% worldwide) late last year after having played 1800 1v1 matches. I’d like to think I’m an active member in the Reddit community, so you might have seen me posting and commenting in /r/starcraft and the allthings subreddits as ZephyrBluu.

And before you ask, yes Zephyrus was named after my username which has also been my gamertag for as long as I can remember.

Even though I don’t have time to play anymore, I still try to keep up to date with GSL, WCS and the general comings and goings in the community. If you want to get in touch to suggest a feature or just have a chat, your best bet is to PM me on Reddit. Otherwise, send an email to hello@zephyrus.gg.

Anyway, that’s enough about me. It’s time to talk a bit more about Zephyrus.

Zephyrus

Starcraft II is a complex game, yet we lack tools for in-depth analysis of both gameplay and player information. Zephyrus is my approach to solving this problem.

Zephyrus is about helping players understand their gameplay better and enable them to both celebrate their strengths and identify their weaknesses.

My philosophy on data is all about simplicity and clarity. I want to provide players with beautiful, clean representations of their gameplay that they can understand at a glance. This is my vision for Zephyrus.

Roadmap

Currently, our focus is on completing the foundations of the site and then unlocking more interesting information stored in replays, but in future we want to analyze player profiles and the playerbase at large. Specifically, we are focusing on adding features related to the following:

  • Foundational Features
    • Session tracking
    • Player progress
    • Zephyr, our premium service

  • Replay Analysis Features
    • Race-specific metrics
    • Army composition
    • Vision
    • Building placement (I.e Proxy's)

    • These are of course, subject to change. A full roadmap of future development can be found here.

Analytics

We want to be transparent with our users about all aspects of Zephyrus and that includes tracking user activity. We use Google Analytics and Hotjar on our website to track traffic and user interactions for internal purposes. We do NOT sell or distribute your personal data/browsing activities to anyone. All data gathered is used purely for internal purposes to help us improve user experience and track our growth.
